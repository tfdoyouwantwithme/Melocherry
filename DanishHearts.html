Cream Danish Hearts


Ingredients

Serves 2 people

Dough
  • 1g Dry Yeast
  • 60ml Water
  • 180ml whole milk
  • 50g sugar
  • .5 tbsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 310g flour
  • 170g unsalted butter
Filling
  • 50g dry yeast
  • 250ml whole milk
  • 15g sugar
  • 50g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 15g butter

  • Step 1

    Mix yeast and warm water Let sit 5–10 min



  • Step 2

    In a bowl combine milk sugar salt egg and yeast mixture Add flour Mix to form sticky dough



  • Step 3

    Roll into 23x33 cm rectangle Place butter over ⅔ of dough Fold like a letter



  • Step 4

    Rotate roll and fold again Chill 30 min Repeat rolling and folding 2× more with chilling



  • Step 5

    For filling heat milk In another bowl whisk sugar cornflour and egg Slowly add hot milk Cook until thick Stir in vanilla and butter Cool



  • Step 6

    Roll dough cut into 10 cm squares Add cream to centre Fold corners in or leave as is. Let rise 30–40 min Bake at 200°C for 15–20 min until golden.
